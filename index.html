MonoDialogue

Tutorial

About

Welcome to the MonoDialogue game. This is a one-player game where the player will play two characters, Glenn and Morgan, simultaneously.

What you will do

The rule is simple. You will write dialogue into the textbox for one of the characters at a time, and they will alternate speaking with each other. Some events might pop up as messages in the story. You may choose to incorporate the events into the dialogue, or ignore the events, just like in real conversations! When writing the dialogue, you can end the dialogue by either clicking the button "speak", or hit "Enter" on your keyboard. Click on the "Start" button if you want to play the real game (it will also hide the sample game)! "Restart" button will restart the whole game.

Time

There is a clock that tells you what is the time in the game. The clock in the game will start at 1:20 pm, and you have until 1:28 pm to talk to each other. The clock will start when Glenn finishes "speaking" the first dialogue that you write into the textbox. When the clock hits 12:28 pm, each character would not be able to speak with each other anymore.

Character Information

The information about each character you play is at the bottom of the page. You would probably have to scroll down to reach there. Other than the information listed below, feel free to go in whichever direction you want!

Remark: Hitting refresh on the browser will RESTART the whole game!
